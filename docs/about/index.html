About Me

Ciao, io sono Nico.

I had design training but learned development on my own. The topics are becoming more interesting yet increasingly complicated to study independently.

I remain committed to expanding my expertise to serve others better through a Full-Stack developer course.

My career has been a proud journey of continuous growth. After gaining design knowledge at university, I found a mentor who guided my training as a graphic designer. During this period, I developed a passion for web development.

After three years, my mentor invited me to partner in his firm, where I managed the web and digital department, gaining expertise in both design and development.

å In 2012, I left the partnership to freelance as a consultant, and I'm pleased that many collaborations have continued since then, significantly enhancing my development skills through diverse projects.
